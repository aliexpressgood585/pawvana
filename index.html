Philosophy

A tired nose is a quiet mind

Dogs and cats are wired to sniff, lick, and hunt. When those instincts have nowhere to go, they become anxiety — or your couch.

Ten minutes of focused foraging works a dog's brain like a one-hour walk works its legs. That is the entire idea behind SniffSafari™.

Repetitive licking releases endorphins — nature's own soothing mechanism. ZenLick™ turns stress moments into slow, spa-like rituals.

Indoor cats still carry wild instincts. HuntMode™ offers a safe outlet to stalk, pounce, and win — every single evening.

Questions, answered

How long does shipping take?

U.S. orders arrive in 7–12 business days with full tracking, sent to your inbox within 48 hours. International delivery takes 10–15 business days.

What if my pet doesn't love it?

You have 30 days to change your mind. If it isn't the right fit, write to us and we'll arrange a return or refund — no interrogation.

Are the materials pet-safe?

Yes. Mats and feeders are food-grade silicone and PP, beds use pet-safe plush fabrics, and everything is machine-washable or rinse-clean.

Which size bed should I choose?

S fits pets up to 10 lb, M up to 25 lb, L up to 45 lb, XL up to 70 lb. When in doubt, size up — they love room to sprawl.
